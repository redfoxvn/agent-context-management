# Task Profile: Spike

Use this profile with `skills/acm-task/resources/task.template.md`.

## Purpose

Use this profile for research, exploration, proof of concept, or decision-support work.

## Additional Required Context

Capture:
- research question
- explored options
- findings and evidence
- tradeoffs and constraints
- recommendation or next action
- unresolved uncertainty

## Definition of Ready

Before concluding the spike:
- the research question is explicit
- explored options are distinguishable
- findings are grounded in evidence when possible
- tradeoffs are summarized clearly
- recommendation or follow-up direction is actionable

## Common Mistakes

Avoid:
- presenting assumptions as conclusions
- turning the spike into unbounded implementation work
- omitting tradeoffs or uncertainty
- recording large reasoning dumps without actionable findings
